- Blockchain intro {
    Blockchain is a network of blocks eg. bitcoin, etherium. where anyone can deploy their application or usecase to make decentralise
    and trustworthy.
    - smart_contracts{
        smart_contracts are contracts built by blockchain devs for the particular transaction. it like a agreement between employee and employer, 
        insurance company and you, bank and you, and so on...
    }
}

}
